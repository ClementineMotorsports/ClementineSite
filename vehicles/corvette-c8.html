What is the C8 Corvette like to drive?

The C8 is a mid-engine revolution for Corvette. The 6.2L LT2 V8 sits behind the driver, creating near-perfect weight balance. With the Z51 package, brakes, suspension, differential, and aero are all upgraded for serious performance use.

How does the C8 compare to European exotics?

The C8 competes directly with entry-level European exotics at a fraction of the list price — same mid-engine layout, comparable 0-60 times, and sharper everyday usability. It's America's definitive answer to the supercar.
