Is this a true manual (stick shift) rental in Chicago?

Yes. The M240i has a genuine 6-speed manual gearbox with a clutch pedal — a true stick shift. It is one of the only manual transmission sports car rentals available in the Chicago metro area.

The F22 M240i is widely regarded as one of the last true analog BMWs — lightweight, rear-wheel drive, turbocharged inline-six, and available with a manual gearbox. It's the purest driver's car in the fleet.
