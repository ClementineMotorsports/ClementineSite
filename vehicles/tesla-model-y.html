What is it like to drive a Tesla Model Y?

The Model Y delivers instant torque from a standstill with no gear changes or engine noise. Autopilot handles highway driving with steering, acceleration, and braking. The minimalist interior centers around a large touchscreen with over-the-air updates.

Is charging handled and what about range?

The vehicle is delivered with a full charge. For extended trips, Tesla Supercharger locations are available throughout the Chicago metro and beyond — easily found in the navigation system. Charging details are discussed at booking.

Where is pickup in Chicago?

Pickup is confirmed after booking. We are based in Chicago, IL. Exact location shared upon confirmed reservation.
