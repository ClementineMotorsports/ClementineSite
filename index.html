FAQ

Questions.

Where is Clementine Motorsports located?

We're based in Chicago, IL. After you book, we'll send you the pickup location. Some vehicles are on Turo, others book directly with us.

Can I rent an exotic car in Chicago?

Yes. We have the Lamborghini Urus, Bentley Bentayga, Corvette C8, and the AMG GT C convertible. Requirements vary by vehicle, so reach out and we'll walk you through it.

Do you have a manual transmission (stick shift) car to rent in Chicago?

Yes. The BMW M240i has a genuine 6-speed manual gearbox, three pedals, and rear-wheel drive. It's one of the few real stick-shift sports cars you can rent in Chicago.

Can I rent a Tesla or electric car in Chicago?

Yes. We have a Tesla Model Y Long Range in Chicago. 330 miles of range, AWD, Autopilot. Book it directly on Turo.

How do I book a vehicle?

Some vehicles are on Turo. You can book those directly on the vehicle page. For everything else, send us an inquiry and we'll get back to you within a few hours.

What are the age and license requirements?

Depends on the car. Most are 21+, some of the exotics are 25+. Just ask us about the specific vehicle you're interested in and we'll tell you exactly what you need.

Do you do wedding, prom, or photoshoot rentals?

Yes, and we get a lot of these. Weddings, prom, bachelor parties, birthdays, music videos, photoshoots. Tell us what you're planning and we'll point you to the right car.

Where do you serve in the Chicago area?

We're in Chicago. We've done pickups near O'Hare, downtown, and out into the suburbs. Tell us where you are and we'll work it out.
