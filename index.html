FAQ

Common questions.

Where is Clementine Motorsports located?

We are based in Chicago, IL. Pickup location is confirmed after booking — we serve the Chicago metro area. Some vehicles are on Turo; others are booked directly through us.

Can I rent an exotic car in Chicago?

Yes. Our fleet includes a Lamborghini Urus, Bentley Bentayga, C8 Corvette 3LT Z51, and Mercedes AMG GT C convertible — all available to rent in Chicago. Requirements vary by vehicle.

Do you have a manual transmission (stick shift) car to rent in Chicago?

Yes — the BMW M240i is our manual rental. It has a 6-speed manual (stick shift) gearbox and is rear-wheel drive. One of the few manual transmission sports car rentals in Chicago — and the only one in this fleet.

Can I rent a Tesla or electric car in Chicago?

Yes. Our Tesla Model Y Long Range is available as an electric vehicle (EV) rental in Chicago. Autopilot, AWD, and long range included.

How do I book a vehicle?

Some vehicles are on Turo — click "Book on Turo" on the vehicle page. For others, use our contact form and we'll respond within a few hours to confirm availability.

What are the age and license requirements?

Requirements vary by vehicle. Standard Turo requirements apply (21+ for most, 25+ for some exotics). Contact us directly for specific requirements on exotic vehicles.
